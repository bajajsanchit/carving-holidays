AUSTRALIA

Splendid Australia Tour

Day 01 – Arrive Sydney

Arrive Sydney Airport and transfer to the Hotel. Arrive Sydney Airport and SIC transfer to the Hotel. Afternoon spend around the Darling Harbour. Evening proceed on OWN to King Street Wharf # 5, Darling Harbour by 1850-1900hrs to join Sydney Show Boat Indian Dinner Cruise with Standard Seating (No Transfers – No Jain Menu / does Not operates on TUESDAY & SUNDAY. Overnight at your Hotel.

Day 02 – Sydney

Breakfast. Morning Half Day SIC Sydney City Tour with views of Opera House & Harbour Bridge and visit to Manly Beach, etc Limited Hotel Pickup, No Hotel Drop, this Tour ends by 1145hrs near Darling Harbour or Circular Quay. Get down nearest to King Street, Darling Harbour Proceed to Sea Life Aquarium at Darling Harbour to exchange Combo 2 Pass voucher for admissions to Sea Life Aquarium + Sydney Tower Eye with 4D Experience (Transfers to Aquarium & Tower are Not Included).

Day 03 – Sydney

Day Free to explore.

Day 04 – Sydney - Hobart - Port Arthur

SIC transfer to the airport to board flight to Hobart (Tasmania). Arrive in Hobart and drive to the top of Mt Wellington (1270m) for spectacular views in all directions. After a short drive in the city, head to the Tasman Peninsula for more spectacular coastlines in the Tasman National Park and its sea cliffs from the lookout. Next, see nature’s wonders – the Blowhole, Tasman Arch and Devil’s Kitchen. Overnight at Nubeena/ Port Arthur.

Day 05 – Port Arthur - East Coast Bicheno

Visit Port Arthur Historical Site for a guided walking tour and browse the site to learn about the convict history of Tasmania; then a short visit to a boutique chocolate factory, Federation Chocolates, before leaving the Tasman Peninsula for the East Coast. Next is Swansea – a beautiful East Coast town. At Freycinet National Park view famous Wineglass Bay and more from Tourville Cape Lookout. The tour makes a brief stop at Freycinet Marine Farm if you fancy freshly picked oysters and steamed mussels (own expense), arrive in Bicheno. An optional penguin tour is available in the evening. Overnight in Bicheno.

Day 06 – Bicheno - St. Helens - Launceston

See the amazing Blowhole at the rocky coastline of Bicheno. Visit the multi-award winning East Coast Nature World, a wildlife park. See the granite-rock coastline of the Bay of Fires and one of Tasmania’s best beaches at Binalong Bay. Drive towards Launceston via Scottsdale. On the way, see magnificent St Columba Falls (90m), and carved memorial trees at Legerwood. Arrive in Launceston about 17:30. Overnight in Launceston

Day 07 – Launceston - Cradle

Drive to Cradle Mt National Park via Sheffield, viewing famous lifelike murals and Mt Ronald. There, we walk around Dove Lake and enjoy the view of Cradle Mountain. Then we take a 20-minute easy walk along the creek (Enchanted Walk), view Pencil Pine waterfall, and visit the interpretation center, then return to Launceston via Chudleigh Honey Farm where they offer more than 50+ honey varieties and famous honey ice cream. Return to Launceston at about 17:45

Day 08 – Sydney - Gold Coast

Transfer to the airport to board onward flight to Gold Coast. Arrive Gold coast and SIC transfer to the Hotel. Afternoon, Make your own way to VISIT THE CURRIMBIN WILD LIFE SACTUARY.

Day 09 – Gold Coast

Breakfast. Visit Sea world with SIC transfers. Overnight at your Hotel

Day 10 – Gold Coast - Caines

Breakfast. Checkout & Visit Movie World with SIC transfers. 3 PM transfer to Brisbane Airport to board flight to Cairns. Arrive Cairns Airport & SIC Transfer to Hotel. Overnight

Day 11 – Caines

Breakfast. Full Day SIC Big Cat Cruise Tour to Green Island Reef with Buffet Lunch, Semi-submarine & Glass bottom boat OR Snorkeling Gears (Pak 4). Overnight (Upgrade to Sun lover Outer Barrier Reef Cruise with Semi-Submarine, Glass Bottom Boat, Snorkeling Gear and Buffet Lunch at Supplement Cost Extra of AUD55/Adult & AUD35/Child)

Day 12 – Caines - Melbourne

Breakfast. Depart for Cairns Airport by SIC Transfer for your onward flight to Melbourne. Arrive Melbourne and SIC transfer to the Hotel.

Day 13 – Melbourne

Breakfast. Morning Half Day Melbourne City Tour This Tour ends by 1145hrs at Federation Square. Afternoon full day SIC Philip island Penguin, Kangaroos and Koalas tour. Tour Dep 1300 and return at 2130 hrs Return to Hotel on own. Overnight at your Hotel.

Day 14 – Melbourne

Breakfast. Morning SIC Full Day Great Ocean Road Tour. Arrive back around 8 PM.

Day 15 – Melbourne - Kolkata

Breakfast. Depart to Melbourne Tullamarine Airport by SIC and board onward flight back home.
