EUROPE

Complete Europe

Day 01 - Arrive Prague

Arrival at the international airport of Prague. You will be greeted by our local representatives and transferred to your hotel. The rest of the day is at your own leisure to begin enjoying the site in this wonderful city. We take you to the riverfront by bus and then you will start one hour sightseeing cruise. You will see the most beautiful parts of Prague, the panorama of Prague Castle, the old Charles Bridge, the romantic Kampa Island and the picturesque roofs and spires of the Lesser Town and of the Old Town. The cruise ends at the same pier on the Vltava embankment. This tour is not guided,

Day 02 - Prague City Tour

Breakfast at the hotel. During this excursion we will make you acquainted with the historical district of the city. First, we will show you the New Town (the National Museum, the National Theatre etc.) then our coach (bus) will take you to the other side of the river to the Lesser Town. Next, we continue to the Castle district, where you will take a more than one hour long walking tour, during which you will have a chance to see the world-famous St. Vitus’ Cathedral. Finally, our coach (bus) will take you to the Jewish quarter to admire the synagogues and through the Old Town Square – with the Astronomical Clock and the Tyn-church – to Wenceslas Square, where the tour ends. Guided.

Day 03 - Tour to Cesky Krumlov

Full day coasch tour to the Historical town in Southern Bohemia with the second largest castle in the Czech Republic. Let us invite you to a fairy tale-ish town Ceský Krumlov (situated cca 170 km from Prague), which has been part of the UNESCO cultural and natural heritage since 1992. The town is situated in Southern Bohemia in the romantic area of ponds and beautiful countryside along the river Vltava. In the historical centre there are 300 historical houses and the second largest castle in the Czech Republic with magnificent baroque gardens. There you will see a unique theatre with rotating auditorium. The oldest baroque theatre in the world is part of the castle as well. The historical city centre preserved its medieval atmosphere and picturesque narrow streets. We wish you an unforgettable adventure.

Day 04 - Prague – Day Free at leisure and shopping

Breakfast at the hotel. Explore Prague on your own. Be sure to visit the many boutiques showcasing Prague’s wonderful Bohemian crystal as well as its stunning garnet jewellery.

Day 05 - Prague – Salzburg

Pick yourself drive car from Prague and drive to Salzburg. Arrive Salzburg and transfer to the Hotel. Mirabell Palace, Mozarteum (University of Music), Mozart’s Residence, Trinity Church, Horse Bath, Festival Halls, Hellbrunn Palace (short stop), Frohnburg Palace, Nonnberg Abbey, Mozart Square and ticket for the Fortress Hohensalzburg or Mozart’s Residence.

Day 06 - Salzburg ( suggested tour ) – SOUND OF MUSIC TOUR

Relive with us the making-of the ‚Sound of Music‘ movie in and around Salzburg. This tour, which has been Salzburg’s most popular tour since 1967, is a combination of both historical Salzburg and the breath-taking Lakes and Mountains region. We will show you many of the film locations from the ‘Sound of Music’ and share not only background stories of the movie but also the history and culture of the region. Find out with us whether the hills of Salzburg truly are alive with the ‘Sound of Music’ as you listen to the original movie Soundtrack along the way.

Day 07 - Salzburg – Hallstatt – Salzburg

Breakfast at the Hotel. Visit Hallstatt by yourself drive car. Until the 19th century the beautiful Austrian town of Hallstatt, which overlooks Lake Hallstatt, was accessible only by boat or mountain trail. Today it is a popular tourist destination a UNESCO World Heritage Site, as well as a functioning lakeside town with almost 800 residents. Visit the Kalvarienberg Church / Market Square / Hallstatt Museum etc

Day 08 - Salzburg – Vienna

Self-drive from Salzburg to Vienne ( 295 kms / 3 hrs approximately ). Arrive Vienna and check in hotel. Evening free to explore the cafeterias and markets.

Day 09 - Vienna

Discover the sights of Vienna aboard a double-decker Big Bus as you admire 360-degree views of top attractions and listen to entertaining audio commentary. Use a ticket that’s valid for one, two or three days on two different routes, and hop on and off at any of the 22 stops around the elegant city. Premium and Deluxe tickets include a 90-min guided walking tour as well as the Hundertwasser Tour (Green Route), a unique cabriolet minibus tour to Hundertwasserhaus, the famous expressionist apartment building designed by artist Friedensreich Hundertwasser.

Day 10 - Vienna

Take a drive through the enchanting countryside of western Hungary on this guided coach tour to Budapest. A city rich in traditions, it is situated on the banks of the Danube, where you’ll be given the opportunity to enjoy lunch and see the main sights of the city. Visit Budapest, capital of Hungary, on a day trip from Vienna. See the stunning Hungarian countryside en-route to Budapest.

Day 11 - Vienna

Day Free in Vienna for optional tours or just relax and enjoy the city by yourself drive car.

Day 12 - Vienna – Amsterdam

Self-drive to the airport, drop your car and board your booked flight to Amsterdam.

Day 13 - Amsterdam

Full day Hop on Hop Off tour of Amsterdam including Vollendam / Cheese factory / wind mills.

Day 13 - Amsterdam – Full day Giethoorne Village tour

Giethoorne is a peaceful village in the province Overijssel that is often referred to as the “Little Venice” of the Netherlands, due to its numerous waterways and absence of roads and cars. Its beautiful canals, picturesque wooden bridges and farmhouses with centuries-old thatched roofs give it a unique fairy-tale vibe. The tour will take you from Amsterdam to Giethoorne by bus, where you will cruise the canals by boat and have time to explore the town on your own.

Day 14 - Rotterdam, Delft and The Hague including Madurodam

Drive from Amsterdam for a full day excursion of 9,5 hours to the South of Holland. Visits to Rotterdam and The Hague. Including the Euromast, a Delft Blue Pottery factory, a photostop at the Peace Palace plus a visit to Madurodam

Day 15 - Amsterdam

Day Free at leisure to explore the city center of Amsterdam / the canals / cafeterias and shopping.
