Australia & New Zealand

Day 01 – Arrive Brisbane - Gold Coast

Arrive Brisbane or Gold Coast Airport within 2330hrs and SIC Transfer to your Hotel (Early Check-in is Not Guaranteed). Overnight

Day 02 – Gold Coast

Breakfast. Return SIC transfer for Dream world admission. Overnight at your Hotel

Day 03 – Gold Coast

Breakfast. Return SIC transfer to SeaWorld OR Movie world admission (Choice of 1 Theme Park – must be pre-booked). Overnight at your Hotel.

Day 04 – Gold Coast - Hamilton Island

Breakfast. Checkout & SIC transfer to Brisbane Airport. Arrive Hamilton Island Airport and Courtesy Transfer to your Resort. Your Package includes Free Use of catamarans, paddle skis, windsurfers and snorkeling equipment and unlimited use of the scheduled ‘Island Shuttle’ service (7:00am – 11:00pm daily)

Day 05 – Hamilton Island

Breakfast. Ex Marina Full Day Cruise Whitsunday’s Great Barrier Reef Tour with buffet Lunch, Semi-Submersible, Underwater observatory & Snorkel Gears. Overnight at your hotel.

Day 06 – Hamilton Island - Sydney

Breakfast. Check out and depart to Hamilton Airport by Courtesy SIC. Arrive Sydney Airport within 2100hrs & SIC Transfer to your Hotel and Check-in. Overnight

Day 07 – Sydney

Breakfast. Morning Half Day SIC Sydney City Tour with views of Opera House & Harbour Bridge and visit to Manly Beach, etc (Complimentary & NON Refundable) – Limited Hotel Pickup, No Hotel Drop, this Tour ends by 1145hrs near Darling Harbour or Circular Quay. Get down nearest to King Street, Darling Harbour Proceed to Sea Life Aquarium at Darling Harbour to exchange Combo 2 Pass voucher for admissions to Sea Life Aquarium + Sydney Tower Eye with 4D Experience (Transfers to Aquarium & Tower are Not Included). Evening proceed on OWN to King Street Wharf # 5, Darling Harbour by 1850-1900hrs to join Sydney Show Boat Indian Dinner Cruise with Standard Seating (No Transfers – No Jain Menu / does Not operates on TUESDAY & SUNDAY – No Operations or Not Valid on 03OCT, 25, 26, 27, 31DEC, 01JAN, 26JAN & 14FEB) (Supplement cost of AUD25 Per Person EXTRA to Upgrade to Priority or Window Seating)

Day 08 – Sydney - Blue Mountains

Breakfast. Choice of 1 Full Day SIC Tour – (Limited Hotels Pickup for all Tours) Blue Mountains Tour with Wildlife Park, Scenic Rides at Scenic World – Dep 0800hrs daily / Returns by 1800hrs.

Day 09 – Sydney - Queenstown

Drive to Auckland airport and board flight to Queenstown at 1400 hrs. Please make your own way to Skyline Gondola Ride and one Luge ride located an easy five-minute walk from central Queenstown, Skyline Queenstown offers awe-inspiring views of the Region and an abundance of entertainment and adventure.

Day 10 – Queenstown

Morning makes your own way to the Sky dive office in Queenstown – It takes a certain kind of person to skydive from 12000 sqft. It takes courage. You step out of that aircraft door and for some 25 seconds you free fall at 200km/hour. The personal challenge is immense!

Day 11 – Queenstown

We will leave for Milford Sound – a road trip with breathtaking scenery at every curve and turn so keep your camera handy and no way you can afford a little nap. Afternoon will be spent on Milford Sound Cruise – a cherry on the cake kind of cruise trip after a beautiful journey on Milford Road. We will return to the same hotel for overnight stay at Queenstown.

Day 12 – Queenstown - Auckland

Transfer to Queenstown airport to board flight to Auckland (direct flight) Arrive Auckland,

Day 13 – Rotorua

Rotorua – Matamata – Rotorua – based on Self Drive Own Program. Visit Wai-O-Tapu Thermal Wonderland and 10am daily Lady Knox Falls and Geyser. Eruptions. Polynesian mud bath and spa. Post Lunch around 2 PM Visit the Hobitton. Movie Set. Visit the Hobitton Movie Set from The Lord of the Rings movies in a fascinating.

Day 14 – Rotorua

Self drive to Auckland enroute visiting Waitomo Caves Own Program Waitomo sightseeing. Kiri Te Kanawa once performed. Your tour guide will deliver an Informative and entertaining. Commentary, sharing stories, cultural legends and the natural Wonders of this living masterpiece.

Day 15 – Auckland

City sightseeing based on Self Drive Program (Highlights: Harbour Bridge, Waterfront, and Mission Bay, Tamaki Drive, Savage Points, and Parnell Rose Park and Village, Domain Parks and Gardens, City Centre; Victoria Park)

Day 16 – Delhi

Shuttle transfer to the airport to connect Intl flight back home.
