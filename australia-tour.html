AUSTRALIA

Australia Tour

Day 01 – Arrive Sydney

On arrival in Sydney clear customs and immigration and proceed to the hotel. SIC transfer to the hotel and Check in to the hotel. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 02 – Sydney

Morning SIC tour of the city Visit the Opera House from outside followed by a panoramic city tour which includes Manly Beach, the Harbor Bridge, Macquarie’s Point and drive through King’s Cross. Drop at the Darling Harbour. Later in the evening, board the Show Boat Cruise for dinner and dance while sail through the shimmering Sydney Harbour with its mesmerizing panorama of bright city lights, Opera House and the famous Harbour Bridge. A three course Indian Dinner, a dazzling sensational theater cabaret featuring professional Australian showgirls in their glamorous costumes are all of a part of the evening.

Day 03 – Sydney

Full Day SIC Blue Mountains Tour with Wildlife Park and Scenic Rides at Scenic World – Dep. 08:00hrs daily / Returns by 1800hrs – Limited hotel pickup (Compulsory & NON Refundable)

Day 04 – Sydney - Cairns

Checkout SIC transfer to Airport to board flight to Cairns. Arrive Cairns Airport & SIC Transfer to Hotel. Day Free at leisure. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 05 – Cairns

Full Day: Kuranda Scenic Railway Day trip with rain forest. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 06 – Cairns

Breakfast. Full Day SIC Big Cat Cruise Tour to Green Island Reef with Buffet Lunch, Semi-submarine & Glass bottom boat OR Snorkelling Gears (Pak 4). Overnight at the Hotel. Green Island Day tour includes, Cruise to Green Island aboard “Big Cat” a modern catamaran, Buffet lunch served on board in air-conditioned comfort, Snorkelling demonstration & reef presentation en route to Green Island, Use of Big Cat vessel during time at island as an air-conditioned base, Island rainforest boardwalks.

Day 07 – Cairns - Melbourne

Breakfast. Check-out & SIC transfer to airport to board the flight for Melbourne. On arrival in Melbourne SIC transfer to the hotel and Check in to the hotel. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 8 – Melbourne

Breakfast at the Hotel. Morning city tour of Melbourne. Afternoon Full Day SIC Phillip Island Penguins, Kangaroos & Koalas. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 09 – Great Ocean Road - Melbourne

Full day SIC tour Experience one of the world’s finest coastal drives at Great Ocean Road, see the famous 12 Apostles rock formations including the Lock Arc Gorge. Later drive through the Port Campbell National Park

Day 10 – Departure Melbourne

Breakfast at the hotel. Checkout & transfer to Airport to board flight to Delhi on 03 Jan 0020 hrs.
