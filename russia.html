RUSSIA

All Around Russia

Day 01 – Arrive Moscow

Arrival at the Moscow Airport. A warm welcome by our representative. Transfers to Hotel & relax whole day & explore the city on your own. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 02 – Moscow

After breakfast proceed for Moscow City Tour Panoramic: Lenin’s Tomb (outside), St. Basil’s Cathedral (outside), Red Square, Sparrow hills, Moscow State University, Victory park. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 03 – Moscow

Breakfast at the hotel & enjoy Metro Tour & Arbat Street & Kazan Cathedral. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 04 – Moscow / Saint Petersburg

After breakfast transfers to railway station to board train for St. Petersburg. On arrival transfers to Hotel & relax at the hotel. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 05 – Saint Petersburg

After breakfast please enjoy City tour: State Hermitage Museum (outside), the Admiralty (outside), St.Isaac’s Cathedral (outside), Fine Arts Square, Church of Resurrection on Spilled Blood, Smolny Cathedral, Bronze Horseman. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 06 – Saint Petersburg / Tashkent

Transfers from Hotel to Airport to board flight for Tashkent. On arrival transfers to hotel & time to explore the city on your own. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 07 – Tashkent

After breakfast enjoy Tashkent City Tour (Amir Temur Square, etc.) Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 08 – Tashkent

After breakfast proceed for Full day trip to Charvak Lake & Chimgan Mountains. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 09 – Tashkent departure

Transfers from Hotel to Airport to board flight for final departure with sweet memories.
