JAPAN

Japan Highlights

Day 01 – Arrive Tokyo

On arrival in Tokyo Narita Airport, you will be met, assisted by English speaking assistance for your transfer to Tokyo Hotel by Airport Limousine bus (Seat In Coach transfer). The rest of the day is free.

Day 02 – Tokyo

This morning experience the many sights of Tokyo on a City Tour (Seat in coach tour). See Tokyo Tower, Tea Ceremony Experience of (35 Min), Enjoy Lunch – Japanese style (Veg/Non-Veg), After Lunch Visit – Imperial Palace Plaza, Nijubashi Bridge, Board the Sumida River Cruise, After Cruise ride, visit Asakusa Kannon Temple, Nakamise Shopping Street and the Tasaki Pearl Gallery.

Day 03 – Tokyo - Mt. Fuji - Tokyo

The tour commences from Hamamatsucho Bus Terminal for standard hotels its 5 minutes’ walk for First Class Hotels client will be picked up from hotel. The Tour ends at Hamamatsucho Bus Terminal, Tokyo Station, Shinjuku Station & Ginza Station from where clients have to make their own way to hotel. Visit Japan’s most famous natural attraction – Mt Fuji. At 3776 meters, it is Japan’s highest mountain. Enjoy Lunch – Japanese style (Veg/Non-Veg). Later enjoy cruise on pristine Lake Ashi, and a ride on the Komagatake Ropeway which boasts great views of the surrounding Hakone National Park.

Day 04 – Tokyo - Hiroshima

Today, check out of the hotel and proceed on your own to Tokyo Station. Travel from Tokyo to Hiroshima by JR Bullet train.

Day 05 – Hiroshima

Today enjoy a full day tour of Hiroshima and Miyajima tour (Seat in coach tour). Visit Miyajima, Itsukushima Shrine. A World Heritage site. Established in the 6th century, this shrine is one of Japan’s finest, and is famous for its beautiful Shinden-zukuri style structure (architecture representative of a nobles’ residence in the Heian period). Visit Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park & Museum. This large-scale park was established in 1949 after the Pacific War ended, and is visited by countless visitors as a symbol of peace.

Day 06 – Hiroshima – Kyoto

This morning proceed on your own to Hiroshima Station. Travel by train from Hiroshima to Kyoto. On arrival into Kyoto Station, proceed on your own to hotel.

Day 07 – Kyoto - Nara - Kyoto

The tour commences and ends at the following point from Kyoto Station, Hotel Nikko Princess Kyoto if guest is staying in same hotel, Hotel Karasuma Kyoto, Ana Crowne Plaza Kyoto, Hotel Rihga Royal, Kyoto Okura & Kyoto Tokyu Hotel. Passengers have to make their own way. Visit Nijo Castle, KinkakuJI Temple, Enjoy Lunch – Japanese style (Veg/Non-Veg), Later visit Kyoto Imperial Palace, Todaiji Temple, Nara Deer Park, Kasuga Shrine, Nara Nagomikan.

Day 08 – Kyoto - Osaka

Today check out of the hotel and proceed to Kyoto Bus Station on your own for transfer to Osaka airport by Airport Limobus (Seat in Coach tour) for your flight back home.
