Croatia

Day 01 - Arrive Zagreb – Plitvice Lakes

The first day is a visit to the capital of Croatia, Zagreb. Getting to know the city accompanied by our local guide begins your adventure. You will visit the colorful square of Sv. Marka, famous cathedral of Zagreb, stone gate, as well as other historical sights. After experiencing the magic of the urban city and the metropolis, in the evenings you will have the opportunity to taste the specialties of Zagreb in the famous local restaurants. We are in Zagreb, and we are preparing for the next day, the arrival of Plitvice Lakes.

Day 02 - Plitvice Lakes – Zadar

After breakfast in the beautiful Zagreb we will head for a pleasant ride to the world-famous Plitvice Lakes. This national park is under the protection of UNESCO. On the way to this natural pearl we will stop in the small village of Rastoke, which goes off with pristine nature and surroundings. Plitvice Lakes consist of 16 lakes. This national park is packed with preserved historical mills where you can experience the production of flour. The beauty of waterfalls, lakes, and waterfalls will experience you with a boat ride that will give you an unforgettable feeling. Overnight in Zadar.

Day 03 - Zadar – Split

After breakfast in Zadar, we will visit another national park, Krka National Park. This national park will give you another experience of enjoying waterfalls and lakes. The National Park devours pristine nature, plant and animal life. From this visit to this national park, there will be unforgettable memories and stunning photos that will envy you all the lovers of photography. As part of our visit to this national park, we will take you through the nearby small town of Skradin, which is full of restaurants where you will be able to taste all Dalmatian specialties, from fish, shellfish to ham. After lunch, we head towards the city of Sibenik where we will visit the world famous cathedral of Sv. James who is under UNESCO. After wandering through this tiny, but beautiful Dalmatian town, we will head off the road along the Dalmatian coastline, which will give you an unforgettable view throughout the Dalmatian islands. From all the panoramas, you will most like the view of the fairytale Primošten. Overnight stay in Split.

Day 04 - Split – Klis – Trogir – Split

After breakfast in Split, your valley starts through this pearl jade. He will visit the places where the famous Game of Thrones was shot. Our local guide will take you through the Diocletian’s Palace, which is 1700 years old. The whole palace is full of Roman style construction, basement, temples. They will delight you to visit the impressive Cathedral of St. Dujma, who is also the patron of this city. After a detailed tour of the palace and natural sights we head towards the fort of Klis near the splint on which also the Game of Thrones is being recorded. When you get to this fortress, you will be impressed with the view over the entire city of Split, its surroundings and the dams’ islands. After a historic presentation we start at lunch in the nearby restaurant on Dalmatian specialty, roast lamb. After lunch, we will head for a short ride to the nearby town of Trogir. This small town, full of romantic stone streets, will fascinate you at first glance. The city is also protected by UNESCO. Our guide will take you to visit the Trogir cathedral, Sv. Lawrence. Many of these cathedrals are considered the top of the architecture of Croatia. Before going to Split, you will also have the opportunity to visit the City Museum. In the evening, we return to Split, where you will be able to feel the magic of nightlife in Split. Overnight stay in Split.

Day 05 - Split – Hvar – Split

The fifth day is reserved for the island of Hvar. This island is considered one of the most beautiful islands in the whole Adriatic and you really must see it and feel it. At this adventure we start by ferry from Split harbor where you will enjoy a two-hour voyage to the Adriatic Sea. When we arrive in the city of Hvar, there are several hours to get to know this beautiful island. The island is full of historical sights, Dalmatian life, food, drinks, urban life. This island is an inevitable resort for many famous people, actors, singers. The guide will take you to the famous fort Fortica with a beautiful panoramic view. After sightseeing and strolling the beautiful stone streets, we head for a small drive to the nearby village of Malo Grablje where you will be able to taste the specialties of Dalmatian cuisine. After lunch, we will return to the Old Town where you will have wonderful memories with you. Full of lovely experiences and memories we return to Split, where we will enjoy the evenings in the local restaurants. Overnight stay in Split.

Day 06 - Split – Peljesac – Ston – Dubrovnik

In the morning after breakfast, we leave Split and head for Bosnia and Herzegovina. We are going to visit the enchanting city of Mostar. On the way to the Mostar we will visit the famous walls of Počitelj. This is a historic city that will enchant you on the first. There are many religions, cultures and religions in this city. History is felt at every step. The stone streets are full of souvenir shops. The historic Old Bridge will delight you. The local guide will spoil you with the beauty of the city through an unforgettable walk through the stone streets. You will experience the true charm of the orientee. After a detailed tour, we will dine at a nearby restaurant where you will taste the true Bosnian specialties. After lunch in the afternoon we will head for a short drive to the south of Croatia. They will enjoy driving on the Dalmatian coast. We come to the city of Dubrovnik. Overnight in Dubrovnik.

Day - 07 Dubrovnic tour

After the overnight stay and breakfast, we will embark on an unforgettable adventure where you will discover the beauty of this truly special city of Dubrovnik. The streets of this town depict the history of the Game Of Thrones crew. Along with local guides, walking through the Dubrovnik walls will enchant you the famous promenade, Stradun. The city is full of historical sights, from which stands the Franciscan monastery, the Prince’s Palace, the Orlandi Pillar and the church of St. Vlaha, who is also the guardian of this city. Throughout your free time, you will have the opportunity to explore this world-famous city on the sole side of famous people. After a detailed tour of the city, we will head to the famous Srđ (412m) promenade, which will leave you a breathless view of the city center of Dubrovnik. They will enjoy you in terms of free time until evening evenings when we will go to a farewell gala dinner that will crown this wonderful trip to beautiful Croatia. Overnight in Dubrovnik.
