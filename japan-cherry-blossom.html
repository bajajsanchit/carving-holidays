JAPAN

Japan Cherry Blossom

Day 01 – Arrive Tokyo

On arrival at Tokyo Narita Airport, you will be met, assisted by English speaking assistance for your transfer to Tokyo Hotel by Airport Limousine bus (Seat In Coach transfer). The rest of the day is free.

Day 02 – Tokyo

This morning enjoy Tokyo’s Cherry Blossom Tour. (seat in coach tour) Visit the Showa Kinen Park, continue to the Tokyo Main Tower – Japan’s second tallest structure for a panoramic city view. Later visit the Chidorigafuchi Kitanomaru Park which is the most popular cherry blossom viewing spot. Conclude the tour with a romantic evening Sumida River Cherry Blossom Cruise. Catch breathtaking sights of the magnificent pink blossoms.

Day 03 – Tokyo

The tour commences from Hamamatsucho Bus Terminal for standard hotels its 5 minutes’ walk for First Class Hotels client will be picked up from hotel. The Tour ends at Hamamatsucho Bus Terminal, Tokyo Station, Shinjuku Station & Ginza Station from where clients have to make their own way to hotel. See Tokyo Tower, Tea Ceremony Experience of (35 Min), Enjoy Lunch – Japanese style (Veg/Non-Veg), After Lunch Visit – Imperial Palace Plaza, Nijubashi Bridge, Board the Sumida River Cruise, After Cruise ride, visit Asakusa Kannon Temple, Nakamise Shopping Street and the Tasaki Pearl Gallery.

Day 04 – Tokyo - Mt. Fuji - Hakone

oday, check out of the hotel and proceed for a full day tour of Mt. Fuji and Hakone. Visit Japan’s most famous natural attraction – Mt Fuji. At 3776 meters, it is Japan’s highest mountain. Enjoy Lunch – Japanese style (Veg/Non-Veg). Later enjoy cruise on pristine Lake Ashi, and a ride on the Komagatake Ropeway which boasts great views of the surrounding Hakone National Park.

Day 05 – Hakone - Kyoto

This is the only mountain railway in Japan. The train departs from Hakone-Yumoto station (at 96 m above sea level) and takes about 40 minutes to arrive at the final stop, Gora station (at 541 m above sea level). Halfway up the line there are switchbacks, where the driver and the conductor change shifts and the train switch to reversed travel direction. On arrival at Kyoto Station, proceed on your own to hotel. The rest of the day is free at leisure.

Day 06 – Kyoto

Visit Ninna-ji Temple, Tenryu-ji Temple, Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, Kitano Tenmangu Shrine & Hirano Shrine, Hean jingo Shrine, Okazaki Canal & Kawabata-dori street. The tour commences and ends at city center point, passengers have to make their own way.

Day 07 – Kyoto - Nara - Kyoto

Today enjoy a full day tour of Kyoto and Nara Tour. (seat in coach tour). Visit Kinkaku-Ji Temple, Nijo Castle. Enjoy Local Japanese Lunch. Continue to visit Todaiji Temple, Nara Deer Park, Kasuga Shrine & Nara Nagomikan. Tour concludes at Kyoto Station.

Day 8 – Kyoto - Osaka

Today check out of the hotel and proceed to Kyoto Bus Station on your own for transfer to Osaka airport by Airport Limobus (Seat in Coach tour) for your flight back home.
