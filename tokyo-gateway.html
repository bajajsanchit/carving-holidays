JAPAN

Tokyo Gateway

Day 01 – Arrive Tokyo

On arrival in Tokyo Narita Airport, you will be met, assisted by English speaking assistance for your transfer to Tokyo Hotel by Airport Limousine bus (Seat In Coach transfer). The rest of the day is free.

Day 02 – Tokyo

The tour commences from Hamamatsucho Bus Terminal for standard hotels its 5 minutes’ walk for First Class Hotels client will be picked up from hotel. The Tour ends at Hamamatsucho Bus Terminal, Tokyo Station, Shinjuku Station & Ginza Station from where clients have to make their own way to hotel. See Tokyo Tower, Tea Ceremony Experience of (35 Min), Enjoy Lunch – Japanese style (Veg/Non-Veg), After Lunch Visit – Imperial Palace Plaza, Nijubashi Bridge, Board the Sumida River Cruise, After Cruise ride, visit Asakusa Kannon Temple, Nakamise Shopping Street and the Tasaki Pearl Gallery.

Day 03 – Tokyo

The day is free at leisure to explore the city on your own. OR take an optional tour to visit Japan’s most famous natural attraction Mt. Fuji and Lake Hakone OR visit Tokyo Disneyland.

Day 04 – Depart Tokyo

Today, you will be transferred to Tokyo Narita Airport by Airport Limousine bus (Seat In Coach) for your flight back home.
