EUROPE

Spain

Day 01 - Arrive Barcelona

Arrival to the airport. Private transfer to the hotel.

Day 02 - Barcelona

Breakfast. Hop On and Hop Off as many times as you like at any of the 30 stops at major Barcelona landmarks. Tour stops include the Sagrada Familia, the Casa Batlló, the World Trade Center, the Camp Nou Stadium, the Barceloneta beach and much more. The Barcelona City Tour Bus runs from 09.00 am to 07.00 pm on winter and from 09.00 am to 08.00 pm on summer. Both routes depart and end at Plaça Catalunya but you can hop on and hop off at any bus stop.

Day 03 - Barcelona - Valencia

Breakfast. Transfer to the airport in Ibiza and flight to Barcelona. Arrive to Barcelona and pick yourself drive car to drive to Valencia.

Day 04 - Valencia

Breakfast. Albufera touristic bus and boat ride. Visit Albufera natural Park, a wonderful wetland centred around a large lagoon and served by a hop on-hop off bus service from Valencia. This tour also includes a cruise on the lagoon.Starting point at Plaza de la Reina. You can join the bus at each stop. The end point is the same as starting point.

Day 05 - Valencia - Madrid

Breakfast. Departure to Madrid by self-drive.

Day 06 - Madrid

Discover Madrid from the comfort of our bus. Go on and off as many times as you wish to get to know and fall in love with the city. The Madrid City Tour Bus runs from 09.00 to 22.00, March to October, and 10.00 to 18.00, November to February. Route 1 departs and ends at Prado Museum and is 80 minutes long approx. Route 2 starts and ends at Plaza Neptuno and is 65 minutes long approx. Discounts booklet Madrid City Tour Stops guide.

Day 07 - Madrid - Tenerife

Breakfast. Drop your self-drive car at the airport in Madrid and board flight to Tenerife island. Pick a self-drive car to the hotel.

Day 08 - Tenerife

Breakfast. Day at leisure with self-drive car.

Day 09 - Barcelona

Breakfast. Drop your self-drive car at the airport in Tenerife to board flight to Barcelona. Arrive to Barcelona and connect flight further to onward destination.
